There exists various barriers to export for many small food processing firms. However, the opportunities to establish profitable market outlets in foreign countries by small food processing companies is attractive to many of these firms. This paper reports on the international market assessment of a new meat product. Specifically, a market segmentation analysis of bison was performed in France. Results are provided to assist bison processors/retailers in developing: 1) target customer profiles; 2) specific marketing strategies; and 3) strategies for restaurants, meat stores, and food processors/manufacturers.
