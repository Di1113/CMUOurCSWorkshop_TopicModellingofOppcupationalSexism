A CD‐ROM–based laboratory manual for the torsion experiment in the Strength of Materials Laboratory was developed through collaboration between Civil Engineering and the Interactive Multimedia Department. The labware is intended to enhance student learning through the development of and exposure to richer learning tools, resources, and advanced technologies. © 2000 John Wiley & Sons, Inc.
