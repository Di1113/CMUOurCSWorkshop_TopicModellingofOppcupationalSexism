This study uses multiple methods to deepen an understanding of how outmigration of male family members affects the livelihoods and work patterns of female family members who are left behind in Bardiya, Nepal.

This research is part of the Secure Livelihoods Research Consortium (SLRC) programme

Ghimire, A., Hagen-Zanker J., and Bhujel S. (2019) Migration and work: are women's work patterns changing in Bardiya, Nepal? Working paper. London: Secure Livelihoods Research Consortium.
