(MENAFN - The Conversation) Dr Gosia Mikolajczak is a Research Fellow at the Policy Lab, University of Melbourne. Her research focuses on gender discrimination, ideology, wellbeing, and applications of different quantitative research methods in social research. She is currently working on the ARC Discovery project 'Understanding political debate and policy decisions using big data'. She has published in various journals including Sex Roles, British Journal of Social Psychology, Journal of Social and Political Psychology, and PLOS One.
